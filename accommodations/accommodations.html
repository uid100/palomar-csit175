Persons with Disabilities
For students with any kind of disability, services and support are available to you through the Disability Resource Center (DRC). Call 760-744-1150x2375 or visit the Disability Resource Center (DRC) web page. Please see me if you need any special accommodations such as sitting in a specific seat (I.e., front row), extra time to complete assignments, exams, etc. Even for online classes, there may be accessibility issues we have not considered that need to be accommodated. We won't know unless you choose to tell us.
